Who We Are

Bringing Europe's Hidden
Culinary Gems to Korea

Sweetly discovers exceptional, time-honored food brands across Europe and introduces them to discerning Korean consumers through premium retail and online channels.

Founded in December 2013, Sweetly Co., Ltd. operates as an exclusive importer and distributor of premium European food brands in the Republic of Korea.

We define ourselves as a Premium Food Brand Developer — not merely importing products, but building each brand's identity, awareness, and sales presence in the Korean market through tailored marketing strategies.

From Tuscan olive oil and Neapolitan tomatoes to artisan pasta and aged balsamic vinegar, every product we represent carries a story of craftsmanship and heritage. Once collaboration begins, Sweetly is committed to maintaining the partnership for more than a decade.

Brand Portfolio

Exclusive Partnerships with
Europe's Finest Artisans

Each brand is carefully selected for its heritage, quality, and alignment with our commitment to authentic European gastronomy.

Italy · Tuscany
Martelli Pasta
Legendary artisan pasta from Lari, Tuscany. Family-crafted in small batches, bronze-die extruded, slow-dried for 50+ hours.
Italy · Gragnano
Faella Gragnano IGP
Pasta from the historic birthplace of Italian pasta. IGP certified, ultra-rough surface, 60+ hour slow drying process.
Italy · Tuscany
Caponi Egg Pasta
Handcrafted egg pasta since 1953. Cold-processed to preserve the delicate character of free-range eggs and premium semolina.
Italy · Naples
Casa Marrazzo
San Marzano DOP tomatoes from Vesuvius volcanic soil. Hand-peeled since 1934, Neapolitan tradition at its finest.
Italy · Modena
Villa Manodori
Balsamic vinegar crafted by Massimo Bottura, the world's #1 chef. DOP 12-year aged, Barolo, and artisanal IGP varieties.
Italy · Amalfi Coast
Armatore
Anchovy fillets and Colatura di Alici from Cetara. Traditional chestnut-barrel aging, minimum 8 months maturation.
Italy · Sicily
Scalia
Sicily's finest anchovies from Sciacca coast. Entirely hand-crafted, aged in pure ingredients for rich, clean umami.
Italy · Tuscany
I Tattoli
Chianti Classico DOP Extra Virgin Olive Oil. Hand-harvested, cold-pressed within hours, remarkably low 0.14% acidity.

ASdoMAR
Premium tuna and salmon fillets by Generale Conserve SPA. Firm texture, clean taste, essential for any kitchen.
Italy · Prato

Spain · Andalusia
